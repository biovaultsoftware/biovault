1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the BalanceChain Platform.

Our core principle: privacy by design.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not:

  • collect personal identity data
  • store biometric data on our servers
  • store private keys
  • store transaction histories centrally
  • track users across devices

3. Local-Only Data

Most data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • ledger segments
  • cryptographic proofs
  • biometric authentication material
  • vault state and backups

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

4. On-Chain Data

When using on-chain gateways:

  • only cryptographic proofs and required transaction data are submitted
  • no personal identity data is included
  • blockchain data is public by nature and governed by the underlying network

5. Communications

If you contact us via email:

  • we receive your email address and message
  • we use it solely to respond
  • we do not sell or share communications

6. Cookies & Analytics

The Platform does not use:

  • tracking cookies
  • behavioral analytics
  • advertising pixels

Basic technical logs may exist at the hosting level for security and uptime.

7. Third-Party Services

Wallets, blockchains, and external services you choose to use are governed by their own privacy policies.

We are not responsible for third-party practices.

8. Data Security

Security measures include:

  • local encryption
  • device-level access control
  • biometric authentication (where supported)

However, no system is 100% secure.

9. Your Choices

You control:

  • your data
  • backups
  • exports
  • deletions (by clearing local storage or uninstalling)

We cannot recover lost data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page.
